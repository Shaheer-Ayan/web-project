Healthy Eating Guidelines

- Eat a variety of foods to ensure you get a balanced diet.
- Incorporate fruits and vegetables into every meal.
- Choose whole grains over refined grains.
- Limit added sugars and saturated fats.
- Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Sample Healthy Recipes

Quinoa Salad

This refreshing salad is packed with protein and fiber.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste

Grilled Chicken with Veggies

A simple and delicious meal loaded with nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • Olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper to taste

Fact: Healthy carbohydrates are essential for energy.
