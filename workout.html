Full Body Workout

This workout targets all major muscle groups and is great for building strength.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Upper Body Workout

This workout focuses on building strength in your arms, shoulders, and chest.

Exercises:

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps

Lower Body Workout

This workout is designed to strengthen your legs and glutes.

Exercises:

  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps (each leg)
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
